Background

Hi, I'm Jeffrey Allen — an Electrical and Computer Engineering graduate of the University of Colorado Boulder, class of May 2026. I'm passionate about the full stack of power electronics: from penciling out a topology and running simulations, to soldering components onto a custom PCB and validating the loop gain on a vector network analyzer.

My internship at The Boring Company gave me a real appreciation for industrial-scale electrical work — commissioning a 1.1 MVA substation and developing NEC-compliant distribution panels for tunnel infrastructure. It bridged the gap between textbook power systems and the realities of high-voltage field work.
