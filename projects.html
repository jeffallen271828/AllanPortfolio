Senior Capstone — 100W USB-PD Variable Power Supply

University of Colorado Boulder  ·  Power Design Lead & PCB Designer  ·  Fall 2025 – Spring 2026

Project Overview

Engineered a 100W portable, variable-output bench power supply powered entirely by the modern USB Power Delivery (USB-PD) standard. The system consolidated multiple subsystems onto a single power conditioning PCB to route clean, low-loss power to precision analog stages.

Tags: USB-PD Protocol · Buck/Boost · LDOs · CV/CC Variable Power Outputs

  • Capstone Report PDF

Key Subsystems

  • Power Delivery Input (PDI): Transitioned from an MCU-based approach to a standalone TPS25730D controller to automatically negotiate a 20V/5A (100W) contract. Sustained thermal testing verified peak surface temps stabilized at a safe 34.8°C under maximum continuous draw.
  • Power Rail Generation: Converted the 20V input to stable ±25V rails using an LM5116 inverting buck-boost pre-regulator and a TPS55340RTER boost regulator. Designed dedicated input π-filters to decouple switching stages and attenuate high-frequency transients.
  • Ultra-Clean Output: Fed the ±25V outputs into LM317 and LM337 linear dropout regulators (LDOs) to supply an ultra-clean, noise-suppressed ±20V to the arbitrary waveform amplifier.

CU Hyperloop Team — Tunnel Boring Machine

Electrical & Software Team  ·  August 2024 – September 2025

Project Overview

Developed the electrical architecture, custom PCBs, and embedded firmware for a student-built Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) to compete in The Boring Company's annual "Not a Boring Competition."

Tags: PCB Design · Embedded Systems · Sensor Telemetry

  • Hyperloop Report PDF

Hardware Subsystems

  • Fluids Board: Engineered a multi-channel pump control PCB using a Teensy 3.1. Utilized buck converters and direct drives to provide water cooling and deliver clay-dissolving fluid from the control station to the cutting head.
  • Navigation Board: Designed an aggregation board to capture TBM positioning and drift data utilizing VectorNav-100 IMUs, transmitting data back to the system GUI via Ethernet.
  • Rear Gripper Board: Served as sole designer for Revision A (and circuit designer for Rev B) of a JST-connector-dense PCB. Captured telemetry for temperature, methane, and pressure across the 6-linear-actuator pushing system.

Photovoltaics Power Lab

University of Colorado Boulder  ·  Power Electronics Engineer  ·  Spring 2026

Project Overview

Designed and built a complete 85W photovoltaic power conditioning chain, transforming raw solar energy into a synthesized 120 VAC, 60 Hz grid-level output — covering every stage from PV harvest through DC link regulation to AC synthesis.

Tags: MPPT / Solar · Custom Magnetics · DC-AC Inverter

  • Photovoltaics Report PDF

Key Contributions

  • Custom Magnetics: Fabricated, gapped, and characterized custom power magnetics using N95 ferrite cores for both the buck and cascaded boost converter stages.
  • MPPT Algorithm: Implemented a custom C++ Perturb-and-Observe MPPT algorithm, achieving high converter efficiency at the Maximum Power Point.
  • DC-AC Synthesis: H-bridge stage synthesizes a clean 60 Hz sine wave from the regulated DC link for grid-compatible output.
